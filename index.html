Frequently asked questions

Is Petlio free?

Yes. The core of Petlio — pet profiles, health and weight tracking, reminders — is free to use. Premium unlocks extras, and if you'd rather not subscribe you can open most premium features by watching a short rewarded ad instead.

Which pets can I track?

Dogs, cats, birds, fish and reptiles, with a growing library of 40+ breeds. Each species gets the right fields and care tips, and you can add as many pets as your family needs.

Do I need an account?

No — you can start right away and keep everything on your device. Sign in only if you want to back up and sync your records across devices.

Where is my data stored, and is it private?

Local records stay on your device. If you sign in, your data is securely stored in Google Firebase and protected with App Check. We never sell your data. You can lock private photo albums behind Face ID, fingerprint or a passcode, and delete everything at any time.

Can Petlio remind me about vaccines and medications?

Yes. Set reminders for vaccines, medications and routines — they push to your phone and sync straight into your device calendar so nothing slips.

Is the Petlio AI a replacement for a vet?

No. Petlio AI gives general, pet-aware guidance and points you to a professional when it matters — but it is never a substitute for a licensed veterinarian. For any health concern, see your vet.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual records in the app, or remove your whole account from Settings. You can also email us. Full steps are on the Delete my data page.
